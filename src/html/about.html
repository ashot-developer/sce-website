Snapper Creek Energy: A Data-Driven Approach to Energy Markets

Snapper Creek Energy is redefining the energy brokerage landscape. Our team of industry veterans combines rigorous market research with a proprietary analytics platform to provide unparalleled insights, execution services, and strategic guidance.

Our Footprint

Operating through distinct-but-integrated, highly specialized brokerage desks—Natural Gas, Power, Ags and Softs, Carbon, and NGLs—we serve a diverse global clientele, including banks, macro hedge funds, specialized oil and gas investment funds, trading firms, market makers, and oil companies.

Our expertise extends beyond brokerage, offering clients market transparency, data-driven decision-making, and bespoke advisory services that unlock hidden value in the energy sector. At Snapper Creek, we don’t just facilitate trades—we empower businesses to make smarter, more strategic moves in a dynamic commodity landscape.

Our Team

Sid Perkins
CEO
Sidney "Sid" Perkins is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Snapper Creek Energy, bringing over two decades of experience in the energy industry. Prior to establishing Snapper Creek in October 2023, Sid founded ION Energy Group in 2008, where he served as Managing Partner. At ION, he played a pivotal role in brokering the first-ever natural gas trade listed on NASDAQ and personally brokered over nine figures worth of commissions. Under his leadership, ION generated nearly $1 billion in brokerage revenue and became a market leader across the oil and gas sector.
