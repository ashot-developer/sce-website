Sid Perkins

CEO

  • Sidney "Sid" Perkins is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Snapper Creek Energy, bringing over two decades of experience in the energy industry. Prior to establishing Snapper Creek in October 2023, Sid founded ION Energy Group in 2008, where he served as Managing Partner.

    At ION, he played a pivotal role in brokering the first-ever natural gas trade listed on NASDAQ and personally brokered over nine figures worth of commissions. Under his leadership, ION generated nearly $1 billion in brokerage revenue and became a market leader across the oil and gas sector.

  • Before ION, Sid was a Partner at LCM Commodities, where he launched and oversaw the NGL brokerage desk. Earlier in his career, he managed the NGL business at Bank of America.

  • Sid holds a bachelors degree from Tulane University and is based in Miami, Florida

  • With a career defined by market innovation and strategic execution, Sid continues to shape the future of energy trading at Snapper Creek.
